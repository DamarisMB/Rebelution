ABOUT

Rebelution is a Grammy-nominated reggae-inspired band hailing from Isla Vista, California. Formed in 200, the group is renowned for their uplifting music, which blends reggae, rock, nd roots with introspective lyrics and nfectious melodies. The band’s mission is to spread positivity, unity, and a message of empowerment through their sound.

The original lineup, consisting of Eric Rachmany (vocals, guitar), Rory Carey (keyboards), Wesley Finley (drums), and Marley D. Williams (bass), formed during their college days at UC Santa Barbara. Since then, Rebelution has become a global phenomenon, headlining tours and playing at major festivals such as Bonnaroo, Lollapalooza, & Reggae Rise Up.

Their debut album, Courage to Grow (2007), earned critical acclaim and set the foundation for their genre-defining sound. Subsequent albums like Bright Side of Life (2009), Peace of Mind (2012), and Falling into Place (2016) showcase their evolution as musicians while staying true to their roots

With timeless hits like “Safe and Sound,” “Feeling Alright,” and “Count Me In,” Rebelution continues to inspire a diverse fanbase worldwide. Known for their high-energy performances and authentic connection with fans, Rebelution remains a driving force in the reggae scene, delivering music that transcends barriers and resonates with the heart.
